Our Ask

We are asking you to commit to donate at least £30 on each of the blessed 30 days of the holy month of Ramadan.
By being among the first 300 people to donate at least £30 for the 30 days of the month you will receive a framed brick from the original build of Cambridge Central Mosque with an engraved plaque featuring a name of your choice.
If you choose to donate less, we thank you for your pledge and may Allah reward you immensely.
